Stuffed Shells

Overview

This recipe layers roasted vegetables, sautéed garlic and spinach, and a quick vegan parm. Use optional dairy parmesan if that suits your table. Shells are parboiled, shocked in an ice bath, and baked with marinara until hot and bubbling.

Tip: Buy extra jumbo shells to cover roughly 10% breakage during boiling.

Ingredients

Vegan Parm

  • 1/2 cup raw cashews
  • Nutritional yeast
  • Garlic salt
  • Onion powder
  • Black pepper

Reference: 4 ingredient vegan parmesan cheese

Stuffed Shells

  • Garlic salt
  • Black pepper
  • Jumbo shell pasta
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 sweet potato
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1/2 zucchini
  • Baby spinach
  • Olive oil
  • Pasta sauce (Rao's Marinara)
  • High quality shredded parmesan cheese, optional

Directions

Vegan Parm

  1. Toss all ingredients into a food processor and blend until sandy.

Shells Filling

  1. Preheat oven to 350ºF.
  2. Cut sweet potato, bell pepper, and onion into 1/2" pieces; toss in olive oil and roast 15 minutes.
  3. Transfer to bowl, add 3 tbsp vegan parm, garlic salt, and pepper.
  4. Sauté minced garlic in olive oil, then wilt spinach; add to bowl.
  5. Cook zucchini 3–5 minutes until al dente; add to bowl with optional parmesan.
  6. Mix vegetable filling thoroughly.

Shell Pasta Preparation

  1. Boil shells per package (approx. 15 minutes).
  2. Prepare a cold water ice bath.
  3. Transfer shells into ice bath to stop cooking.

Stuffed Shells Cooking

  1. Preheat oven to 350ºF.
  2. Coat 11x13 dish with pasta sauce.
  3. Fill shells with mixture and place in dish.
  4. Top with sauce and bake 15–20 minutes.

Notes

  • Rao's Marinara offers a bright tomato flavor.
  • For vegan service, skip dairy parmesan.
  • If shells crack, layer filling with broken shells for a baked pasta variation.

Source for vegan parm inspiration: Sweet Simple Vegan.
